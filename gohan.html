PERSONALITY

Gohan may be small, but he is mighty! Gohan is the son of Goku and his human wife Chi-Chi, which makes him half saiyan and half human. As a result, he often doubts his abilities. His human side suppresses his saiyan strength but don't make him angry or his wrath will be unleashed! Gohan is the warrior with great potential but he doesn't realize it; he's a ticking time bomb! Gohan often begins fights with underwhelming odds until he's pushed to his limit; he then reaches unbelievable power levels which enable him to overpower his opponent. Gohan's special move is the Kamehameha!
