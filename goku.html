PERSONALITY

Kakarot, also known as Goku, is arguably the most powerful saiyan in the universe! Don't let his jovial nature fool you; This saiyan can overcome all odds. Goku posesses an odd sense of humor at times, but he can be serious when he needs to be. Goku's appetite is about as large as the love that he has for his family and friends. Goku finds fun in challenging those who he deems stronger than him, which is how he constantly pushes himself to his limits. As a result, Goku always surpasses his limits and reaches new power levels!
