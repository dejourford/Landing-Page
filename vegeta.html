PERSONALITY

Vegeta is the blood brother of Goku, whom he refers to by his Saiyan name, Kakarot. Vegeta is also from the pure Saiyan bloodline like Goku. Vegeta is high-tempered and easily angered and it shows in his fights. He is just as might as Goku but has trouble reaching his full potential. His potential is powered by the jealousy that he has towards Goku surpassing him in Saiyan power. He is an absent father to a half-saiyan and half-human son named Trunks. Vegeta isn't much of a talker, but don't make him angry or else he has the potential to release his wrath upon you! Vegeta's special move is Galick Gun!
